# Recent BDHS hypertension competitors (for Discussion positioning)

Retrieved 2026-06-08 via Perplexity + verified author/year via PLOS pages.

- Das et al. 2025, PLOS ONE 20(10):e0335442. "Predicting hypertension and identifying most important factors among married women in Bangladesh using machine learning approach." BDHS 2017-18, 4,253 married women, HTN prevalence 23.1%. Twelve ML algorithms under six class-balancing strategies; SHAP interpretation. Best: Extra Trees + SMOTE+ENN, ROC-AUC 0.95. This is the canonical "resampling + subpopulation" ML competitor; its high AUC is not directly comparable to a full-sample interpretable model.
- Ghosh et al. 2023, PLOS ONE 18:e0292989. "Prevalence and determinants of hypertension among older adults in Bangladesh." Hierarchical mixed-effects sequential Poisson regression on BDHS 2011 and 2017-18. A prior BDHS multilevel hypertension modeling comparator.
- Pre-hypertension in Bangladesh: evidence from BDHS 2022 (PMC13106122, 2026). Survey-weighted logistic + ensemble ML (Random Forest robustness). Uses the BDHS 2022 wave; pre-hypertension outcome (not diagnosed HTN).
